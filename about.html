About Tyler Chou

Twenty Years in Hollywood. Now Fighting for Creators.

Tyler Chou brings two decades of entertainment law experience to the creator economy. As lead counsel on 18 feature films and former counsel at Disney, Skydance, BuzzFeed, Loeb & Loeb, and Hogan Lovells, she's seen the industry from every angle.

She left a 20-year Hollywood career to stand up for creators—to protect them from bad deals, to help them build empires, and to ensure they keep what's theirs.
