history

The Godmother of Creators

Tyler Chou spent two decades as an entertainment attorney at Disney, Skydance, BuzzFeed, and top law firms like Loeb & Loeb and Hogan Lovells. She served as lead counsel on 18 feature films and advised companies worth billions.

Then she walked away from all of it.

Why? Because she watched creators get exploited by the same systems she helped build. Bad deals. Stolen IP. Empires handed over for pennies.

Now she fights for the other side.
