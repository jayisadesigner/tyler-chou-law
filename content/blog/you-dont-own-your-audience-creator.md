From YouTube.

From Instagram.

From TikTok.

That channel you've spent years building?

That audience you talk to every day?

Gone.

Because the algorithm changed.

Because you got hacked.

Because your content "violated community guidelines."

Or because of the feared copyright strikes.

And just like that—

your life's work disappears.

I've seen it happen to creators with millions of followers.

Overnight.

No way to reach their audience.

No backup.

No email list.

No land of their own.

## Do You Own an Email List?

Lastly, do you own an email list with your audience?

If you do not, start today. It is THE most valuable commodity you own as a creator.

Offer a free checklist or PDF.

Or tell them you are starting a newsletter and would like to add them to your list.

An email list is like gold. It only appreciates and is priceless.
