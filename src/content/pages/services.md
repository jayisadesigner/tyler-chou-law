        answer: "Federal trademark filings start with USPTO fees per class (currently $350 per class) plus our flat-fee legal work. We quote a single, transparent fee tied to the scope of the brand portfolio — not hourly billing. Most creator brands need 1–3 classes for the launch portfolio."
      - question: "Can I be sued by an old employer over my channel content?"
        answer: "Possibly — if your former employment agreement had a broad IP-assignment or non-compete clause that reached the work you're now doing. We review the original agreement, draft a clean break, and structure your current entity so future revenue lives outside any prior claim."
